Why We Use Lean Body Mass (LBM)

Protein builds and repairs muscle — not fat. Calculating protein based on your LBM (estimated from height + weight using the Boer formula) gives a far more accurate target than total body weight, especially if you carry extra fat or have a heavier/lighter build.
